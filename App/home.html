A life form evolves, sustains, and reproduces. A most incredible aspect of a living system is that it is made up of non-living atoms and molecules.

Biomolecules have been essential for the proper functioning of living organisms. Biomolecules include a variety of macromolecules (protein, carbohydrates, nucleic acids, and enzymes) and small molecules (amino acids, vitamins, fatty acids, neurotransmitters, and hormones).


This project was undertaken with the aim of contributing to the improvement of understanding of the principles that underlie biological dynamics and functions.
